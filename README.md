## Introduction

[Neuroscience](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neuroscience) is the study of how the nervous system develops, its structure, and what it does. Neuroscientists focus on the brain and its impact on behavior and cognitive functions.

[Computational neuroscience](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computational_neuroscience) is a multidisciplinary science that joins biology/neuroscience, medicine, biophysics, psychology, computer science, mathematics, and statistics to study the nervous system using computational approaches.

## Learning Resources

### Ebooks & Textbooks
- **[Neuroscience Online](http://nba.uth.tmc.edu/neuroscience/m/index.htm)** - Open-access electronic textbook covering neuroscience in depth. (UT Houston)
- **[Computational Cognitive Neuroscience](https://grey.colorado.edu/CompCogNeuro/index.php/CCNBook/Main)** - Introduction to biologically based computational models.
- **[Neuronal Dynamics](https://neuronaldynamics.epfl.ch)** - Open-access textbook on computational and theoretical neuroscience. (EPFL)
- **[Andy's Brain Book](https://andysbrainbook.readthedocs.io/en/latest/)** - Companion to Andy's Brain Blog, covering fMRI analysis and neuroimaging tools.
- **[NiPraxis](https://textbook.nipraxis.org/intro.html)** - Textbook covering fundamental concepts in neuroimaging analysis.

### Blogs
- **[Neuroskeptic](https://www.discovermagazine.com/author/neuroskeptic)** - Critical look at developments in neuroscience, psychiatry, and psychology. (Discover Magazine)
- **[The Neurocritic](http://neurocritic.blogspot.in/)** - Critical takes on sensationalistic findings in brain imaging and psychopharmacology.
- **[The scicurious brain](https://blogs.scientificamerican.com/scicurious-brain/)** - In-depth analysis of single research papers. (Scientific American)
- **[Action Potential](http://blogs.nature.com/actionpotential)** - Forum operated by neuroscience editors at *Nature*.
- **[Andy's Brain Blog](https://www.andysbrainblog.com/)** - Collection of articles, tutorials, and videos on neuroimaging tools.

### MOOCs
- **[The Fundamentals of Neuroscience](https://www.mcb80x.org/)** (Harvard & edX) - Introductory survey of topics in neuroscience.
- **[Introduction to Neuroscience](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/brain-and-cognitive-sciences/9-01-introduction-to-neuroscience-fall-2007/)** (MIT OCW) - Structure and function of the human brain.
- **[Computational Neuroscience](https://www.coursera.org/learn/computational-neuroscience)** (Coursera) - Introduction to computational methods for understanding nervous systems.
- **[Medical Neuroscience](https://www.coursera.org/learn/medical-neuroscience)** (Coursera) - Functional organization of the human central nervous system.
- **[Neuromatch Academy](https://github.com/NeuromatchAcademy/course-content)** - Jupyter notebooks from a summer school in computational neuroscience.
